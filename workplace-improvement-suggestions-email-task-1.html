Task 1

You have been working at your current company for two years and have noticed some areas where the workplace environment could be improved. You believe these changes would boost employee morale and productivity.

Write an email to your company's Human Resources Manager in about 150-200 words.

Your email should do the following things: -

  • Identify two specific areas that need improvement in the workplace.
  • Explain how these issues are affecting employee morale and productivity.
  • Suggest practical solutions to address these problems and improve the work environment.
Dear Nicole,

I am writing regarding workplace environment.

This month I am celebrating my two-year anniversary at our company, and I really enjoyed my tenure here. During my time here, I have noticed, we could renovate the library meeting room. Moreover, the pantry area has old equipment which takes a long time to warm up the meals.

Most staff members use the library meeting room for small size meetings. During discussion employees raised their concern to have improved wall paint and lightning. At present the paint is quite dark and lights are warm white, which makes overall room light as dark. Furthermore, microwave in kitchen is of low voltage. It takes ten minutes to warm the meal which makes the break time short.

As a result, management should consider these concerns on a high priority, redecorate the meeting room and upgrade the appliances. This would make our staff positive and they can put more productivity. Additionally, staff will have more time to relax during lunch time which will make them energetic for the rest of the shift.

Thanks for attention to this matter. I look forward to see the improvements soon.

Sincerely,
Mark Thomas
